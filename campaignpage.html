Campaign Page

Places like the Los Angeles Mission and Anne Douglas Womens Shelter provide clothes, food, and shelter for people living on Skid Row. They are even given the opportunity to enter a faith-based program that helps them rejoin society. During the holidays, there is an increase in support, but there are not enough volunteers year-round. You do not have to be a rich, famous celebrity to donate and do your part. Donating old clothes or volunteering your time has just as big of an impact.
